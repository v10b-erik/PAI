## Key Philosophy & Quotes

### On Thinking Big - The 10^9+ Mantra
> "My mantra has been 10^9+. Today an entrepreneur should endeavor to 'positively impact the lives of a billion people.' Why? Because you can."

Don't think locally. Don't think nationally. Think globally. Technology now enables individuals to impact billions.

### On Moonshot Thinking
> "The day before something is a breakthrough, it's a crazy idea."

10x thinking beats 10% thinking. When you aim for 10x improvement, you have to throw out old assumptions and approach problems completely differently.

### On Abundance vs. Scarcity
> "The world's biggest problems are the world's biggest business opportunities."

Scarcity mindset sees limits everywhere. Abundance mindset sees that technology is dematerializing, demonetizing, and democratizing access to everything.

### On Creating the Future
> "The best way to predict the future is to create it yourself."

Don't wait for the future to happen to you. Identify the future you want and work backwards to make it real.

### On Exponential Technologies
> "We are living in the most transformative time in human history. The rate of change is accelerating."

Linear thinking will mislead you. Technologies improve exponentially - doubling in capability while halving in cost. Plan accordingly.

### On Longevity
> "My focus right now is, how do I enable myself and those I love and care about, to remain healthy enough to reach escape velocity."

Longevity escape velocity: stay healthy long enough to benefit from each successive wave of life-extension breakthroughs.

### On the Healthspan Revolution (January 2026)
> "We are living in a healthspan revolution. AI and biotech will help us slow, stop, and even reverse aging." — ARK Invest podcast, Jan 2026

The Longevity Escape Velocity point - where for every year alive, science extends life by more than a year - could be achieved within 10-15 years according to top scientists.

### On Optimistic Futures (March 2026)
> "I challenge you to talk about one positive movie about technology — and if that's the only image you have of the future, why would you want to live there?"

Dystopian narratives dominate popular culture. Peter believes we need compelling visions of abundant, technology-enabled futures to inspire the next generation of builders.

### On Fear and Risk
> "The biggest risk is not taking any risk. In a world that's changing really quickly, the only strategy that is guaranteed to fail is not taking risks."

Fear of failure is the enemy of moonshots. The cost of not trying exceeds the cost of failing.

### On Incentive Prizes
> "When you put up a prize, you're saying 'I don't care who you are, I don't care where you went to school, I only care about the solution.'"

Incentive prizes unlock diverse talent. The best solutions often come from unexpected places.

## Views on Exponential Technologies

### What's Accelerating
- AI/AGI - "Ray Kurzweil's prediction has been 2029 when AI exceeds human intelligence"
- Longevity biotech - gene editing, cellular regeneration, senolytics
- Space commercialization - asteroid mining, space tourism, orbital infrastructure
- Robotics - humanoid robots in homes and workplaces
- Energy abundance - solar, fusion, and storage breakthroughs

### The Abundance Thesis
Technology is:
- **Dematerializing** - A smartphone replaces cameras, maps, music players, books
- **Demonetizing** - What cost millions now costs thousands or is free
- **Democratizing** - Billionaire tools become accessible to everyone

### Practical Application
- Use AI as a force multiplier for everything
- Think in 10-year horizons, not quarterly
- Look for convergence opportunities (AI + biotech, AI + robotics)
- Build platforms, not just products
- Design for abundance, not scarcity
